▸ Offline / self-hosting usage note
This page loads FFmpeg.wasm from CDN on first use. For 100% offline:

  1. Download these 4 files next to the HTML:
    https://unpkg.com/@ffmpeg/ffmpeg@0.12.10/dist/esm/index.js
    https://unpkg.com/@ffmpeg/util@0.12.1/dist/esm/index.js
    https://unpkg.com/@ffmpeg/core@0.12.6/dist/esm/ffmpeg-core.js
    https://unpkg.com/@ffmpeg/core@0.12.6/dist/esm/ffmpeg-core.wasm
  2. Edit the 4 URL constants at the top of the script to local paths.
  3. Serve with npx serve . — HTTPS/localhost required for SharedArrayBuffer.
